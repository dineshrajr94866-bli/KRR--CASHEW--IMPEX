Process

From farm gate to container.

Every consignment moves through the same five stages, so quality stays consistent whether you're ordering one pallet or twenty containers.

01

Sourcing

Raw cashew nuts sourced from growers and regional suppliers, selected for moisture content and shell condition.

02

Processing

Steaming, shelling, and drying at partner processing units under hygienic, monitored conditions.

03

Grading & Sorting

Kernels sorted by size and quality into standard trade grades — W180 through W450, plus piece grades.

04

Packing

Vacuum-sealed or nitrogen-flushed packing in export cartons, labelled by grade, batch, and weight.

05

Export

Documentation, container loading, and shipment on FOB or CIF terms to your destination port.
